In any case, it would be nice if there was another opportunity to write more episodes in the past where the protagonist and high elf show more emotion and are constantly bickering.

To everyone who helped make this book happen, I extend my thanks. And also to all the readers, thank you for picking up my book. Until the next book, Thank you very much.

All the best.

Fujino Omori
