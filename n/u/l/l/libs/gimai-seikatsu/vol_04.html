As the streetlamps came on that evening, the scenery was calming, rather than hot and oppressive. Shinjou slowed down so much, I thought our long, long shadows might even catch up with us. Finally, he came to a complete stop, and I was forced to stop, too.

The other resident got on the elevator and eyed me suspiciously as they went up and up. I waved, then sighed.

I really have it bad.
