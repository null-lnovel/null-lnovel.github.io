Someone unforgettable-just as Kasuka, whose name could also be read as "Yuu," was to Yuu's own parents.

Do you mean you have a thing for me? The thought made Sora's cheeks flush as Kaede continued.

Nobody knew how this quartet's story would unfold from here on. The romantic comedy had only just begun-and so the protagonists marched on to their futures, no matter whether it proved to be a comedy or tragedy to them all…

Ha-ha-ha! So you fools thought this was a side story? And so A Sister's All You Need. comes to its real, true, honest end. I pray that the futures of everyone who read this to the end is as happy as possible, but even more than that, I want to be happy. I wanna get married, dammit!!!
